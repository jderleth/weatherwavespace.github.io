Constellations Working Together

Weatherwave Space, Inc. builds cooperative small-satellite systems that achieve flagship-class performance through intelligent distribution. By designing spacecraft to work as a synchronized network instead of isolated vehicles, we unlock new dimensions of spatial, temporal, and spectral resolution for Earth and planetary observation.

Our Approach About Us

Synergizing Performance

Flagship missions prove what’s possible; distributed systems make it practical. Our constellations scale capability through cooperation—sharing data, timing, and tasks to deliver persistent global coverage and rapid revisit.

Continuous Improvement

Progress shouldn’t wait for a decade-long mission cycle. Weatherwave follows a lean engineering model: launch, learn, iterate. Each generation of vehicles incorporates lessons from the last, creating steady technological ascent rather than periodic leaps.

Resilience by Design

We embrace the logic of the Internet: replication beats fragility. Every node adds redundancy, adaptability, and fault tolerance—ensuring that our sensing networks remain operational even when individual elements fail.
