Privacy Policy

Weatherwave Space, Inc. respects your privacy and does not track, collect, or share personal data through this website.

Effective Date: November 2025
Last Updated: November 2025

1. Overview

Weatherwave Space, Inc. (“Weatherwave,” “we,” or “us”) respects your privacy. We do not track, collect, or share personal information through this website. This site exists solely to provide general information about Weatherwave’s mission and work.

2. Information We Collect

We do not use cookies, analytics tools, or third-party advertising systems. The only information we may receive is any that visitors voluntarily send through normal communication channels. Such correspondence may be retained temporarily to allow a response and is deleted when no longer needed.

3. Information We Share

We do not sell, rent, or share any information with outside organizations or individuals, except as required by law or to protect our legal rights.

4. Security

This website uses standard encrypted (HTTPS) connections. However, no online system is completely immune from interception or misuse. Visitors should avoid sending confidential or proprietary information through unencrypted means.

5. Links to Other Sites

This website may contain links to other organizations or resources. We are not responsible for the privacy or security practices of external websites.

6. Policy Changes

If Weatherwave’s operations evolve to include services that collect user data (e.g., newsletters or accounts), this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly. Any material change will be reflected on this page with a new “Effective Date.”
